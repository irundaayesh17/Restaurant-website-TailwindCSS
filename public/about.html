About Us

At Food House, we pride ourselves not only on our delicious and healthy menu but also on our commitment to creating a welcoming dining experience. Nestled in the heart of Colombo, our cozy bistro is a favorite gathering spot for locals and visitors alike. Our passion for Mediterranean and Middle Eastern cuisine shines through in every dish we serve. From our homemade hummus and flavorful falafel to our vibrant salads and satisfying wraps, each item on our menu is crafted to deliver a taste sensation that keeps our guests coming back for more. Beyond our culinary offerings, we emphasize sustainability by sourcing ingredients from local farmers and suppliers who share our dedication to quality and freshness. Our cozy ambiance and attentive service ensure that every visit to Food House is not just a meal, but an enjoyable and memorable experience. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch break or settling in for a leisurely dinner with friends and family, we invite you to savor the flavors of the Mediterranean and Middle East right here at Food House.
